# Nội dung Review

**Propositional Logic**:
- Truth tables, connectives, implications, tautologies, equivalences, DNF/CNF.

**Predicate Logic**:
- Quantifiers ∀ ∃, predicates, negation.

**Proof Techniques**:
- Direct proof, contradiction, induction.

**Counting**:
- Addition/multiplication, permutations, combinations, pigeonhole, inclusion-exclusion.
